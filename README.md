# OA Grant Design & Fidelity Toolkit

## Purpose

The OA Grant Design & Fidelity Toolkit helps GII teams design, implement, monitor, and improve grants in alignment with the Opportunity Accelerator (OA).

---

## Grant Lifecycle Components

### 01. Strategy & Opportunity Framing
Assess opportunities before pursuing funding.

### 02. Grant Design
Develop theory of change, logic models, KPIs, and service alignment.

### 03. RFP & Site Selection
Ensure member selection processes align with OA expectations.

### 04. Implementation Planning
Prepare sites, staffing, reporting, tools, and TA supports.

### 05. Monitoring & Fidelity
Monitor performance, implementation quality, and OA fidelity.

### 06. Closeout & Learning
Capture lessons learned and continuous improvement opportunities.
